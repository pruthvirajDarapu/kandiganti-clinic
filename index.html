Welcome to Kandiganti Clinic

About

At Kandiganti Thalli Pilla Clinic, our mission is rooted in compassion, trust, and excellence. We believe that every child deserves a healthy start and every woman deserves personalized, respectful care throughout every stage of life. As specialists in pediatrics and obstetrics & gynecology, we bring years of experience and dedication to the families we serve. Whether it's a newborn's first check-up, a young child’s vaccination, or a mother’s journey through pregnancy and beyond, we are here to guide, support, and heal. Our clinic is more than a place of treatment—it is a space of comfort, understanding, and hope. We are committed to staying updated with the latest medical advancements while maintaining the warmth and accessibility that our community values. We thank you for placing your trust in us and look forward to being a part of your family's health and happiness.

Offering Services

Children's Specialist Services: - Fever, cough, cold, and other illnesses - Newborn baby care - Vaccinations - Nutritional advice - Growth and development monitoring - Asthma, allergy, and other respiratory problems - Skin diseases - Epilepsy, fits, and other neurological problems

Women's Specialist Services: - Normal and cesarean deliveries - Infertility treatment - Menstrual problems - Uterine fibroids, ovarian cysts, and other gynecological surgeries - Family planning services - Adolescent health problems - Menopause-related issues - Cancer screening and treatment
